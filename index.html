Welcome to FlowState!

FlowState is a real-time collaborative art project. Open this page in another browser tab, on a smartphone/tablet, or send the link to your friends. Everyone's touches appear on the shared fluid canvas.

Use FlowState to calm yourself, "dance" with your socially-distant friends, or as a shared drawing space. Click Get A Room to create your own canvas and share the link with collaborators. Shared Settings affect the appearance/behavior of the canvas for everyone while My Settings affect only the touches you add.

FlowState is a work in progress. Send Grant thoughts, suggestions, and bugs. FlowState also outputs to an LED wall as a socially distanced interactive art installation.
